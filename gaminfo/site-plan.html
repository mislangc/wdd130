Purpose

This site provides helpful information of specific games such as it's enemies, weapons, tactics, where can you play it and many more. This site free and is accessible to everyone around the world. Informations found in this site can help you beat levels, stages and bosses so that you can relax and enjoy the game you are playing with your friends and even with your family.

Audience

For struggling gamers out there and discovering new games, this site is the place you want to be.

Content

Home page

Games are around the world. Games can be fun, competitive, stress relieving or time consuming. Games are outside the world and inside technologies. This site can help you find games that you want to know more about.

Different platforms of games. PC or personal computer is the basic form of technology where you can use a keyboard and a mouse. Mobile phones are flexible where you use a keypad or the screen if it is touchscreen. Mobile can be used anywhere you like, and you can play inside or outside your homes. Playstation uses a game controller called joystick you can play the game you want by inserting the game disk in the playstation. Xbox has the same function as playstation but it uses a more complicated joystick where you can also play the via a game disc or through the internet.
