Rivers

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

Salmon River, also known as "The Salmon", was given the nickname as the "River of No Return" because of the river's rugged beauty, folks that came to visit fell in love that they never left. Herds of elks and deer, bear and bighorn sheep are commonly sighted around the river.

Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah

Desolation Canyon is recognized as a National Landmark. One run in this desolate land takes about an 84-mile trip and is famous for it's high grey stone cliffs, beautiful desert scenery and great rapids.

The Grand Canyon, Arizona

The Grand Canyon is filled not only with exhilirating whitewater rapids, but with side canyons accessible only by river. It is the grand daddy of all river trips. You will never forget your visit to the Grand Canyon.
